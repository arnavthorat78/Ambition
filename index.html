What is Ambition?

Let's see what ambition is!

Ambition is simply a desire to do something, especially something to do in the future, as your job.

Synonym: Aspiration
Now that we understand what ambition means, let's see how I live with it everyday!

Live with Amibition

Let's see how I live with my chosen school value: ambition!

Below is a colourful grid area where I describe in different sections how I live with ambition.

Practice and Do It

If you want to live with your ambition, you should actually do it! This can be done as a hobby during your free time. So, once you have mastered your ambition, you can confidently to what you really want! So remember, practice makes perfect!

Tell People

If you feel like it (and even if you don't, it is recommended to), then you should tell people such as your family, teachers, and close friends about your ambitions. Why? Because people can set you tasks to complete, and they can possibly be of good use for them.

Presenting

This is similar to telling people, except that you will present to many people. You can give some way for contact, so that they can give you projects.

Contribute

Contributing is a great way to see how you feel like in a job-like envrionment. This can be helpful when you start actually getting jobs, since you can get practice in interviews, and the actual projects!

That is how I live with ambition; through the four points.
